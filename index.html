Michael Gira

american instrumentalist & musician

1954

birth

Michael Gira was born on February 19th, 1954 in Los Angeles, California.

1964 - 1969

rebellion

As an adolescent, Gira was arrested in California several times for petty crimes.

1969

escape

Facing the risk of being incarcerated in a juvenile hall, he relocated with his father to Germany

1970

incarceration

Gira ran away and hitchhiked across Europe, lived in Israel for a year, and spent four and a half months in an adult jail in Jerusalem for selling hashish.

1971 - 1978

return

Returning to California, Gira worked at a bakery and completed his GED, went to community college, and then attended an art school in Los Angeles.

1979

purpose

Gira moved to New York in 1979, where he played in a band called Circus Mort, before forming the band Swans.
